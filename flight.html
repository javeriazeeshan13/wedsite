How to Book Your Flight?

Booking your flight with Travelistan is a breeze! Download our app from playstore or app store .Follow these easy steps to secure your next adventure.

1. Search for Your Flight

Start Your Journey: Enter your departure city and destination in the search fields.
Select Travel Dates: Choose your preferred travel dates.

2. Compare Available Options

Browse Flight Choices: After your search, you’ll see a comprehensive list of available flights from various airlines. Refine Your Search: Use our filters to narrow down options by airline, price range, flight duration, and layover times. This makes it easier to find the flight that best fits your schedule and budget. View Flight Details: Click on each flight for detailed information, including baggage policies, in-flight amenities, and more. Make informed decisions based on your preferences.

3. Book Your Selected Flight

Choose Your Flight: Once you find the perfect flight, click “Select” to proceed with your booking. Enter Passenger Information: Fill in the necessary details for each traveler, including names, contact information, and any special requests. Review Your Booking: Double-check all the entered information to ensure accuracy before proceeding. Secure Payment: Choose your preferred payment method and complete the transaction. Our secure payment gateway ensures your financial details are protected.

4. Receive Your Confirmation

Get Your E-Ticket: After successful payment, you will receive an email confirmation with your e-ticket and flight itinerary. This will serve as your travel document. Prepare for Departure: With your flight booked, start planning your journey! From packing essentials to exploring your destination, you’re one step closer to your adventure.
